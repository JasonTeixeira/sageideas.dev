## The Mobile Testing Problem

- **iOS:** 14, 15, 16, 17
- **Android:** 10, 11, 12, 13, 14
- **Devices:** iPhone 12/13/14/15, Samsung S21/S22/S23, Pixel 6/7/8

That's **20+ combinations**. Manual testing took 2 days per release.
